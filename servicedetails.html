Laut Bercerita

Circulation Services

Laut Bercerita tells about the cruelty and brutality experienced by student activist groups during the New Order. Not only that, this novel also reflects on the disappearance of 13 activists, even until now no one has received any clues.

The story in the novel Laut Bercerita is divided into two parts with a very different time interval. The first part is told from the perspective of a character named Biru Laut and his fellow activists as they complete their vision or goals. While in the second part, the story is taken from the perspective of Asmara Jati, Laut's younger brother who has goals or visions that tend to be different from Laut's.
